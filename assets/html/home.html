Symptoms of Brain Stroke

Loss of Balance

Difficulty in maintaining balance and coordination while walking or standing.

Eye Problems

Sudden vision changes, including blurred or double vision.

Extreme Fatigue

Unusual tiredness and lack of energy throughout the day.

Speech Disturbance

Slurred speech or difficulty in speaking clearly.

Terrible Headache

Severe and sudden headaches with no known cause.

Lack of Concentration

Difficulty in focusing or staying attentive.

Making your brain healthy

Best Care For Your Brain

Taking care of your brain is essential to prevent strokes and maintain cognitive health. Stay physically active, eat a balanced diet rich in nutrients, manage stress levels, monitor blood pressure, avoid smoking, and get regular check-ups. A healthy brain leads to a healthier life.

  • Regular Exercises.
  • Visiting doctors in case of slightest inconvinience.
  • Checking your BMI on regular basis.
  • Avoid Alcohol Consumption.
